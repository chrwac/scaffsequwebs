Markov Sequence

The recommended approach is to first provide a training sequence (e.g.: the sequence of the original, mp13mp18-derived scaffold ). A random sequence will be generated that shares some statistics with the training sequence

Markov order

If, for example an order of 1 is chosen, then the generated sequence will roughly (up to some randomness) share the letter frequency of the training sequence. If an order of 2 was chosen, then the generated sequence will share the letter-to-letter transition probabilities with the training sequence and so on.
